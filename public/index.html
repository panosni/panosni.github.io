Panayiotis Nicolaides

I am the Director of Research at the EU Tax Observatory, Paris School of Economics. I specialise on the economics of taxation, working on both policy and research.

Previously, I worked at the European Commission and, as special economic advisor to finance ministers during the Greek economic crisis. From 2016 to 2019, I served as Alternate Member to the Eurogroup Working Group and as Alternate Director at European Stability Mechanism’s Board of Directors.
